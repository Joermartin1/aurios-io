The Problem

Every AI you've ever used forgot you the moment you closed the window.

01
No Memory. No Context. Every Time.

Every session starts from zero. Your history, your preferences, your work — gone. You re-explain yourself to every AI, every time.

02
Your Data Belongs to the Platform. Not to You.

Everything you share with an AI is owned and monetized by the company behind it. You are not the customer. You are the product.

03
You Shouldn't Have to Know Which AI to Ask.

The right model for your question might not be the one you're logged into. The best answer requires knowing all of them simultaneously.

And because it knows you, it can take any question or problem you have and draw on every major AI in the world simultaneously — without you needing to know which one to ask.
